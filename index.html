We Are

Wetrocomposite

WETROCOMPOSITE
is a LEADING

FRP manufacturing
company

We manufacture FRP using Hand Lay-up and filament winding processes. We
design, manufacture, supply, and install items made of FRP (GRP), PVC-FRP, CPVC-
FRP, PP-FRP, PPH-FRP, PVDF-FRP, EPOXY, PHENOLIC, and FURANE. We are looking
ahead to transform UAE into a key strategic regional hub for FRP manufacturing.

Quality Engineered Wetrocomposite

We are a leader in Composite manufacturing industry with one of the largest full-
service networks of custom molding and assembly facilities in UAE. Wetrocomposite
has helped its clients develop and improve their products, efficiency, and bottom
line. We deliver excellence in manufacturing, design engineering support, R&D and
program management. Our Best in Class quality and management practices make us
a trusted partner.
